Children's birthdays

For the little ones: a real workshop,
not entertainment.

We created a unique format: "Il Cibo in Tutti i Sensi" (Food in All Senses). A workshop where children discover food through touch, color, and smell. They paint with fruits and vegetables, they feel, they smell, they create. It's not entertainment — it's an educational experience that becomes a party.

Perfect for birthdays of children aged 4 to 12. Snacks prepared by our kitchen (nothing industrial — everything made with seasonal fruit from our garden), a safe outdoor space for free play, and the workshop as the main activity. Parents sit at the farm bar with an aperitivo, chat, enjoy the valley. Everyone happy.

We build each party to measure: 10 to 25 children, 2–3 hours, snacks, workshop, take-home creative kit.

The kitchen

Chef Simone Cipriani.
From Michelin stars to the earth.

Two Michelin stars, St. Regis, Gordon Ramsay, Gambero Rosso Channel. And then the choice: to leave it all behind and coordinate the projects closest to his heart — a future where food does good to people and to the planet.

Valle Benedetta embraces his vision: plant-based, seasonal cooking, no shortcuts. Every dish comes from the garden, every food choice supports those who produce with respect. It's not just cooking. It's an act of care.

Two floors, multiple settings, one orchestration.

Upstairs room — 80 sqm with floor-to-ceiling glass opening onto a 50 sqm panoramic terrace. Up to 60 seated. Full natural light, view of the Livorno hills. Configurable for plenary, U-shape, or workshop.

Ground floor room — The RistorOrto in day mode: workstations, brainstorming, convivial lunch under white arches and windows on the green. 35–40 seated indoors, up to 80 in fair weather when it opens onto the outdoor deck.

Two rooms, two floors. Keynote upstairs, workshops downstairs. Lunch in between. Aperitivo on the terrace. Everything flows in one place, no transfers, no time lost.

Professional equipment, zero surprises.

Latest-generation interactive touchscreen. Integrated audio system. Video projector. Satellite-grade wifi — tested for international calls, HD streaming, cloud presentations. Arrive, plug in your laptop, it works.

Nine rooms in the villa, for those who want to stay.
